HOW TO COOK PRIME RIB

Prime rib is one of the sweetest dishes if cooked well.Here are some easy to follow steps on while cooking prime rib.

Ingredients

    Pork prime shoulder rib.
    Tumeric.
    Ginger.
    Blackpepper.
    Soy sauce.
    Barbeque sauce.
    Garlic pounded.
    Salt.

Directions.

Add soy sauce with abit of water in a bowl and mix with the spices;in a ratio preffered by you.Stirr thoroughly and dip the ribs inside the mixture ensuring all sides are coated.

Refrigirate for 4-8hours. Remove from fridge and apply soy sauce using a brush before placing on grill.

Let it cook for 15 minutes on each side and ccassinally sprinkle salty water to add flavor and prevent meat from drying.

Apply barbeque sauce and grill for another 3 minutes on each side.

Serving

Served glazed as a main dish or side dish.
